Squid Game Guard Costume

Help control the Squid Game and keep the players in line while wearing this Triangle Guard Jumpsuit costume! This costume features a full hooded jumpsuit with a mock collar, faux pocket detailing, adjustable belt with functional buckle and a triangle guard mask. This costume will have you looking like you just got done monitoring the latest Squid Game competition! Disguise is a leader in the Halloween industry creating innovative and trend setting costumes and accessories. Its team is comprised of a group of gifted in-house designers, sculptors and graphics artists from various creative backgrounds including Hollywood movie sculpt design, the fashion industry and gallery-showcased fine arts. Headquartered in San Diego, Disguise produces costumes and accessories under many of the world’s leading licensed brands as well as its own proprietary brands, for the nation’s largest retailers including specialty, party and pop-up stores.
