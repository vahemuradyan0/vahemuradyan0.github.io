Squid Game Square Mask

Want to play a game? All you have to do is play a few games for kids and you could win big cash prizes... Yeah, right. Nothing's ever that simple! One second you're playing hopscotch, and the next second mayhem and destruction enfold, as your weird, mask-wearing captors enforce crazy rules. We'll take a pass on the games, but we do have an express interest in those masks! If you're looking to dress up like a guard, then you might be ready for one of our Squid Game Square Masks. They're the perfect piece for getting the game started! This Squid Game Square Mask is a black plastic mask that recreates the look of a simple fencing mask. It has a perforated front for vision, along with a white square on the top. The mask fits with an elastic band around the back. You can pair it up with a pink hoodie or jumpsuit to become a deadly gaming guard or pair it up with our circle and triangle version for a great group costume.
